About FreshGuard

Our Mission

FreshGuard is an innovative platform designed to help households and businesses track food expiration dates efficiently. Our mission is to reduce food waste by providing smart tools to manage your pantry, fridge, and freezer contents.

How It Works

The system allows you to:

  • Add products with their expiration dates
  • Get notifications when items are nearing expiration
  • Track your consumption patterns
  • Generate reports on your food waste reduction
